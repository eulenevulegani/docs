3. House Sales Analysis


In this analysis, I delved into crucial aspects of the real estate market. Firstly, I examined the average house prices, offering a detailed breakdown by year and month. This insight into pricing trends over time is invaluable for understanding market fluctuations. Additionally, I explored the price distribution, shedding light on the affordability landscape and market pricing patterns. By breaking down prices based on the number of bedrooms, I provided targeted information catering to the varying needs of potential buyers.
Moreover, the inclusion of interactive slicers allowed for dynamic filtering by time, size, and condition of the houses. This interactive feature enhances the usability of the data, enabling users to customize their analysis and extract pertinent information tailored to their specific requirements. Overall, this analysis equips stakeholders with a comprehensive understanding of the Kingstone housing market, empowering them to make informed decisions in the dynamic real estate landscape.
